Building vibrant, supportive communities one brick, one home and one neighbourhood at a time.

a place for everyone

Sixty Bricks is a dynamic and creative property development company wholly owned by the London Borough of Waltham Forest Council. We take our corporate responsibility seriously; we have a passion and deep conviction for challenging convention.

We ensure that a proportion of our homes are designated as ‘wheelchair accessible’ and are adaptable for multiple needs. Our approach means our homes are designed in such a way that the look and feel of each of our properties are developed the same, whether it’s rental or by private purchase form.
